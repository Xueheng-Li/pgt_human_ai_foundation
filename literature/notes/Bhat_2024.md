## Core Contribution

Studies **value alignment** between humans and robots:
- Operationalized as dynamic goal alignment
- Effects on trust and team performance
- Challenges assumption that alignment always benefits team

---

## Key Concepts

### Value Alignment
- Degree to which robot goals match human goals
- Dynamic: Can change during task
- Observable through behavior

### Trust in HRI
- Trust modulates acceptance
- Affected by alignment perception
- Can be over-trust or under-trust

### Key Finding
> "Prior literature on value alignment makes an inherent assumption that aligning the values of the robot with that of the human benefits the team. This assumption [is challenged]."
