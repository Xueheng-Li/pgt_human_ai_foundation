- **Guilt aversion**: Disutility from disappointing others' expectations

## 7. Theoretical Gaps to Address

Based on this review, the project should address:

### Gap 1: Asymmetric Belief Structures
- No existing work models games where some players have belief-dependent preferences and others have design-dependent objectives
- **Project contribution**: Attributed Belief Equilibrium (P3)

### Gap 2: Heterogeneous Population Dynamics
- Stochastic stability theory assumes homogeneous populations
- Mixed human-AI with different mutation rates unexplored
- **Project contribution**: Multi-speed stochastic stability (P1)

### Gap 3: Spite Acceleration
- Mei et al. (2024) + Li (2026) imitation dynamics suggest AI may accelerate spite
- No formal analysis exists
- **Project contribution**: Spite acceleration theorem (P2)

### Gap 4: Experimental Evidence
- No experiments test human punishment of AI defectors
- No data on norm contagion from AI to humans
- **Project contribution**: Pilot experiment
