## Key Innovations by Project

### P1: Multi-Speed Stochastic Stability
- Order of limits analysis for heterogeneous mutation rates
- Type-dependent resistance counting
- Critical threshold for AI population share

### P2: Spite Acceleration Paradox
- Prosocial AI accelerates spite equilibrium
- Conditional AI design prevents acceleration
- Optimal AI prosociality characterization

### P3: Attributed Belief Equilibrium
- New solution concept for asymmetric ToM
- Attribution function formalization
- Design implications for AI presentation

### Pilot: Empirical Evidence
- Indignation asymmetry (H-A vs H-H punishment)
- Norm contagion from AI to humans
- Threshold effects of AI population share
